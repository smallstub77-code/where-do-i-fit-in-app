Privacy & Data Use

Transparency about what information is collected, how it is used, and how anonymous reports are retained.

Introduction

Where Do I Fit In? is designed to collect body measurement information without requiring registration or personal identification.

Participation is voluntary and restricted to adults aged 18 years or older.

By submitting information through the questionnaire, participants contribute anonymous data that may be used for statistical analysis and research into body size perception and measurement distributions.

What Information Is Collected?

  • Age range
  • Measurement unit preference (cm or inches)
  • Body measurements entered by the participant
  • Self-perception responses
  • Measurement confidence rating
  • Date of submission
  • Generated report information

Not all measurements are required. Participants may submit only the information they are comfortable providing.

What Information Is Not Requested?

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Postal address
  • Government identification
  • Account registration
  • Photographs

Participation is possible without creating an account.

Anonymous Report Identifier

Each generated report receives a randomly generated identifier.

WDFI-7K3M-P9Q2

This identifier is used only to distinguish reports and is not linked to a personal profile.

Why Information Is Stored

  • To generate the participant's report
  • To preserve an anonymous copy of the generated report
  • To improve statistical reference data
  • To support aggregated statistical analysis

Information is analyzed in aggregate form whenever possible.

How Information Is Used

Individual responses are not published.

Results may be included in:

  • Anonymous statistical summaries
  • Aggregate datasets
  • Future research reports
  • Improvements to the reference database

No attempt is made to identify individual participants.

Data Retention

Anonymous records may be retained for long-term statistical analysis and historical comparison.

Because no direct personal identifiers are collected, records are stored using anonymous report identifiers.

Consent

Before generating a report, participants must confirm that:
  • They are at least 18 years of age.
  • They have read the measurement instructions.
  • They understand how submitted information is stored and used.
  • They consent to the anonymous storage of questionnaire responses and generated reports.
